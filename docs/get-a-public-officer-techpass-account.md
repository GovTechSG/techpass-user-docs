# Public officer TechPass account


**Important notes**

- If you don't know what type of TechPass account you are eligible for, please read [TechPass account types](techpass-account-types) before proceeding further.
- If the domain and format of your organisational email address indicate that you are eligible for a **public officer account** and if you have a **non-SE GSIB** device, you may sign up for TechPass. Alternatively, you may also get invited to TechPass.
- If the domain and format of your organisational email address indicate that you are eligible for a **public officer account** but you don't have **non-SE GSIB** device, please submit a [service request](https://go.gov.sg/techpass-sr) or contact another public officer in your agency to get invited to TechPass.
