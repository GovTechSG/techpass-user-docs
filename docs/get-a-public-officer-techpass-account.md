# Public officer account

You can sign up or get invited to get a public officer TechPass account if you are eligible.

**Important information**

- If you need to know what type of TechPass account you are eligible for, please read [TechPass account types](techpass-account-types).
- If you have a **non-SE GSIB** device, you can sign up for TechPass. Alternatively, you may also get invited to TechPass.
- If you are an **SE GSIB** device user, please submit a [service request](https://go.gov.sg/techpass-sr) or contact another public officer in your agency to get invited to TechPass.
